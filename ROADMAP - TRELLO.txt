LIST: SHOULD — High Priority After Launch
================================================================

AI Fridge Scan Flow — Full Redesign (Core Feature)
The current scan flow dumps all detected ingredients into search and likely fails. Needs a full conversational redesign.

Better flow:
1. User scans fridge (up to 5 photos)
2. App shows detected ingredients — user confirms or removes items
3. App asks: "Want to search with all of these or add filters first?"
4. If search fails due to too many ingredients: "Try fewer ingredients, or here are some recipes that use some of what you have"
5. If search succeeds: results shown
6. On results: "How many people are you cooking for?" — portion scaling applied
7. "Want to check you have everything first, or go straight to the recipe?"
8. If checking: show ingredients checklist — user taps anything they are missing
9. For missing items: "Add to shopping list?" — creates a separate meal-specific shopping list just for this meal
10. If user replaces an ingredient: AI adjusts the recipe steps to match the substitution
11. Final verdict: "You're good to go!" or "Might be tricky — here is what you're missing"

This entire flow could be a single Claude API prompt that handles steps 4 through 11 intelligently.

Portion scaling stepper
Add a stepper to recipe detail so users can scale ingredient quantities for 2, 4, or 6 people. All ingredient amounts recalculate automatically. Premium feature. Also needed inside the fridge scan flow.

Missing ingredient — meal shopping list
When a user identifies a missing ingredient during the fridge scan flow, offer to create a separate shopping list just for that meal. Separate from the main shopping list.

Claude-powered step adjustment for substitutions
When a user replaces or is missing an ingredient, use Claude to adjust the recipe steps to reflect the substitution or workaround.

Recipe collections
Let users organise favourites into custom named folders — "Weeknight dinners", "Date night", "Batch cooking Sunday". Creating more than 1 collection is premium only. First collection is free.

Full nutrition breakdown panel
Currently only calories, protein, carbs, fat. Add a full panel inside recipe detail showing fibre, sugar, fat breakdown, RDA percentages. Premium feature.

Dietary profile
Let users set their dietary restrictions once in their profile (gluten-free, vegan, dairy-free, etc.) and have those filters automatically applied every time they search. Premium feature.

AI chat assistant
A conversational screen where users describe what they have or what they want and get recipe suggestions from Claude. "Something quick with chicken", "high protein after gym", "use my leftovers". More flexible than the scanner. Premium feature.

Smart recommendations
Suggest recipes based on the user's tried recipes and favourites history. Low API cost, high perceived value.

Pantry tracking
Manual ingredient inventory. Highlight items approaching expiry. Auto-suggest recipes that use what is about to expire. Big differentiator.

Redis caching
Reduce Spoonacular API calls. Lower costs at scale.

CloudFront CDN
Faster image delivery globally.

Analytics
Track searches, saves, completions, premium conversion, trial retention, meal plan usage, shopping list engagement.

LIST: FUTURE — Phase 3
================================================================

Supermarket shopping list integration
Once the in-app shopping list is generated from a recipe or meal plan, let the user send it directly to an online supermarket for delivery or click-and-collect. One tap from the app shopping list to a populated supermarket cart. Target UK supermarkets: Tesco, Morrisons, Aldi, Lidl, Sainsbury's. No public cart APIs exist for these retailers today — this requires either a partnership or enterprise deal with the retailer, or an aggregator like Instacart if they expand UK coverage. Track Instacart UK availability. This is Phase 3 and partnership territory, but a genuine differentiator worth pursuing once the core product has traction.

Fridge photo to full pantry state
Already partially built — ingredient scan exists. Full "what can I cook with this?" flow using full pantry state. Needs Spoonacular Cook plan ($29/mo) at scale.

Collection management
Recipe boards, folders, custom collections. More of a web feature.
